About us

Our Story

We are a team of highly motivated tech professionals. We use our knowledge, expertise and various talents in tech to solve real problems facing the everyday man. One of our solutions is this platform called ChunkIT, a platform that helps professionals working with large files to better manage their files easily and hassle free.

Our Objective

We aim to solve problems regarding issues of importing and exporting large files. Users of the ChunkIT platform should be able to easily chunk their files and optionally save them on our platform for future download. In addition, the user should be able to view statistics of previously chunked files and sort their files easily on our dashboard. Our platform currently processes CSV and JSON files only; however, we aim to accomodate more file formats.
